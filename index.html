About

Pocket Penguin is an instrumental duo established by Ayumi Toyama and Ryusuke Koarashi in 2011. Pocket Penguin has been mainly focusing on exploring possibility of combination of two intimate instruments—melodica & guitar—by both composing and adapting various music from all over the world. They have had performances in UK, Japan, Italy, Azerbaijan, and Turkey until now. The 1st album “A Bird Flying over Mountains” was released in March 2012, and the 2nd album “Prelude” was released in May 2013. They founded London melodica ensemble in 2013 and have been developing a number of repertoire for the ensemble.
Pocket Penguin (ポケットペンギン) とは、遠山麻友美と小嵐龍輔の２人で、ロンドンを拠点に活動しているインストゥルメンタルデュオです。鍵盤ハーモニカとギターという組み合わせで現代世界の様々な音楽を取り上げると同時に、過去の作品の異なった解釈の可能性を探求します。現在までに、イギリスをはじめ、日本、アゼルバイジャン、トルコ等で演奏しました。2012年にファーストアルバム、”A Bird Flying over Mountains“が、2013年にセカンドアルバム”Prelude“がリリースされています。

Profile

遠山麻友美 Ayumi Toyama

(melodica/piano)

Born in Nagoya, Japan, into a musical family, she began learning classical piano at the age of 4. She graduated from University of Colorado, Boulder in the U.S and now based on London as piano teacher, accompanist and melodica player. Her piano teachers include Naoshiro Kato, Mioko Kato and Piero Quaglia.
